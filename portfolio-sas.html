Student Administration System

Project Summary
 This system is built for a teaching institute to store details like students and admin info; I helps keep and manage the record of factors like personal details, courses, lecture scheduling, billing and transactions. Thus reducing the hours that are lost by the admin in manually generating and managing the records.
